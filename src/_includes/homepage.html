Ricardo Gonzalez


Hello, I am Ricardo Gonzalez. I am a finishing my 2nd year of my PhD in the Information Science Department at Cornell Tech, Cornell University.

In this website you will find many things: My previous work done before joining the PhD, my current projects in Accessibility, and various other posts related to subjects that I like to talk about. You can also take a look at my personal notes and thoughts on general topics.

My research interests lie in designing useful interactive systems focusing on empowering people with disabilities. As of today, I have worked together with people with visual impairments, including blind and people with low vision, to improve the accessibility of 3D virtual environments, AI powered systems, and mobile cameras. Furthermore, my colleagues and I have come up with novel ways of interacting in Virtual Reality, and 3D printed models using Augmented Reality.
